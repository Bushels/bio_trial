# Sound Script — Buperac × SixRing 2026 Farmer Explainer

**Video:** `out/bio-trial-farmer.mp4` — 30 s / 30 fps / 1920 × 1080
**Sound philosophy:** diegetic, tactile, no music bed. The video lives on "the agronomist's desk," so the audio world is paper, marker, stamp, typewriter — with a subtle analog→digital shift in Acts 4–5 when we move from the field journal to the Telegram bot and scoreboard.

---

## Overall mix targets

| Track | dB target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Master | -14 LUFS | loudness target for web/social |
| Foreground SFX (stamps, marker strokes, bubble pops) | -6 to -3 dBFS peak | these are the narrative beats |
| Background SFX (typewriter taps, ambient rustle) | -18 to -12 dBFS | should feel "under" the foreground |
| Room tone / desk ambient bed | -30 to -25 dBFS | optional, glues everything together |

All SFX should be **mono** except the Telegram pops and scoreboard whooshes (stereo is fine there). Avoid anything with strong music-note content — it'll clash with the rhythm of the visuals.

| Sound type | Where to look | Search terms |

| Typewriter | Freesound.org, Pixabay | "typewriter single key", "manual typewriter" |
| Rubber stamp | Freesound, Zapsplat | "rubber stamp", "office stamp", "stamp impact" |
| Marker / pen / highlighter | Freesound | "felt tip pen", "whiteboard marker", "highlighter" |
| Paper rustle | Freesound, SoundBible | "paper flutter", "paper handling", "note card" |
| Pencil scribble | Freesound | "pencil writing", "pencil on paper" |
| Odometer / mechanical click | Freesound | "odometer click", "counter tick", "ratchet" |
| Phone thunk | Freesound | "phone on desk", "phone drop wood" |
| Telegram-style pop | Freesound, Zapsplat | "message pop", "notification chirp", "ui pop" |
| UI whoosh | Freesound | "ui whoosh short", "dashboard whoosh" |
| Carriage return ding | Freesound | "typewriter bell", "carriage ding" |

**Licensing checklist before publishing:**
- Prefer **CC0** (public domain) — no attribution needed
- **CC-BY** is fine for social but keep an attributions file
- Avoid anything marked "non-commercial" (CC-BY-NC) — this video is commercial
- If you use Epidemic/Artlist/Uppbeat SFX packs, keep the subscription receipt handy
